The Best Movies According to Gaby

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Summer Wars

It's a heart warming story about a family coming together to stop a crazed virus from unaliving them with a bomb.

Drive

It was just a really cool movie where it felt like nothing was happening and the visuals were very gorgeous.

Armaggedon

It's just a fun turn your brain off kind of movie. I'm a sucker for disaster movies so this take may be a bit bias.

Resident Evil

It's also just a fun dumb action movie. Who doesn't love Zombies?
